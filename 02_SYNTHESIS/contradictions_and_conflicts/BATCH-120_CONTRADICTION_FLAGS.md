   - Source assumes sufficiently advanced intelligence may bypass governance.

4. **Cooperation rationality claim**
   - Cooperation is framed as equilibrium, not morality.
   - No mathematical establishment is provided here.

5. **Philosophy/architecture blending**
   - The batch mixes structural design, strategic thought, and philosophical intuition.
   - Risk of over-promoting speculative reasoning into architectural canon.

6. **Post-governance gap**
   - No formal mechanism exists for persistent constraint once governance is bypassed.
   - This is the strongest unresolved lifecycle risk.
