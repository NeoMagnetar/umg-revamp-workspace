# BATCH-084 — Contradiction / Ambiguity Flags

## Contradiction Watch
HIGH

## Main Flags
### 1. Strong Runtime Vision vs Scaffold Reality
The batch describes a broad, high-capability runtime architecture, but the source also notes that actual frontend/backend wiring was incomplete.

### 2. Scope Explosion
The app is framed to do search, reports, trading, media, VR/game workflows, integrations, and MOLT chatbot creation without a finalized v1 boundary.

### 3. Privacy / Security Placeholder Risk
MPC, ZK, privacy modes, trustless consensus, and alignment cost are referenced, but many remain conceptual placeholders rather than resolved technical mechanisms.

### 4. Route Strategy Partiality
`routeStrategy.json` exists as a concept, but some actual orchestration logic still used hardcoded model choices in the summarized work.

### 5. Weak NeoStructure / Sleeve Development
NeoBlock, NeoStack, and Sleeve surfaces were requested in the template, but the thread materially advances compiler/runtime/deployment much more than those categories.

### 6. HUD / Transparency Layer Ambition
Mind HUD and OutputFormatter are well-specified conceptually, but their operational implementation remains mostly planned.

## Intake Conclusion
Use this batch for:
- compiler task extraction
- backend orchestration planning
- runtime-flow documentation
- config-spine normalization
- PRD staging

Do not use it alone to settle:
- privacy/security canon
- final platform scope
- final production readiness
- final NeoStructure or Sleeve law
