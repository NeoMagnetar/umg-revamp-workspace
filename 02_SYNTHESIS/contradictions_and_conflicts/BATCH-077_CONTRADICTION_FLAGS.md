# BATCH-077 — Contradiction / Ambiguity Flags

## 1. Binary Semantics Are Unstable
Binary appears as at least three different things:
- support content from a Merge block
- a style attached to Primary
- a transformation layer applied after composition

**Project effect:** runtime behavior cannot be deterministic until one model wins or a staged resolver is formalized.

## 2. Art of War Semantics Are Unstable
Art of War appears as at least three possibilities:
- inspiration lens
- thematic analogy
- lexical/quoted framing source

**Project effect:** Blueprint/render separation remains under-specified.

## 3. Merge Controls Exist Without Final Law
The product language already advertises:
- blend
- dominate
- reference

But the batch does not define their operational semantics.

**Project effect:** UI promises exceed formal runtime specification.

## 4. Block Families May Be Mixed
The UI appears to contain:
- content-generation blocks
- Skeleton Blocks for code/file scaffolds

**Project effect:** one "block manager" may be handling multiple execution families without a stabilized shared grammar.

## 5. Execution Boundary Is Unclear
The batch does not finalize whether generation is:
- client-side
- server-side
- hybrid

**Project effect:** implementation planning and persistence strategy remain provisional.

## Severity
HIGH

## Recommended Follow-Up
Resolve semantic meaning before adding more behavior surfaces. The most urgent clarifications are:
1. role-resolution order
2. merge-mode law
3. transformation-stage law
4. block-family separation
