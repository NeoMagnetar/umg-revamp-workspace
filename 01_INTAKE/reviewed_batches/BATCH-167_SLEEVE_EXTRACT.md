# BATCH-167 — Sleeve Extract

## Core Sleeve Signal
The source defines a layered progression:
- nCube = atomic modular unit
- nStak = recursive subsystem
- nSleeve = complete body of design
- Envoy = sleeve coupled with sovereign mind, continuity, and voice

## Sleeve Definition
A NeoSleeve is presented as an entire plan or product definition. It is complete in design terms, but not yet the full living identity by itself.

## Resleeving / Continuity Signal
The source contributes continuity language rather than explicit protocol language:
- ported
- suspended
- resummoned
- re-instantiable

This implies that sleeve-level persistence and identity continuity can survive transitions across runtime surfaces.

## Overlay Relation
- overlays modulate the surface
- overlays do not rewrite the spine

This is a strong sleeve-governance constraint.

## Envoy Relation
Envoy appears as the identity-continuity-voice layer that emerges when a sleeve is coupled with sovereign cognition.

## Review Note
The sleeve model is conceptually strong here, but strict formal relationships between sleeve, mind, stack, and envoy remain provisional.
