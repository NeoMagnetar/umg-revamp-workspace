## Independent Review
This batch is useful because it gives the project a language for talking about **what kind of intelligence UMG produces**. That is valuable for documentation, positioning, and future taxonomy decisions. It is less useful for concrete runtime law, because many of its strongest claims are conceptual rather than operational.

The most important intake fact is that MCA is explicitly marked as draft and non-canonical. That means this batch should be treated as a candidate identity framework, not a settled one.
