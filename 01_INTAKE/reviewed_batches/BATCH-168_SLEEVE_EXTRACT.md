# BATCH-168 — Sleeve Extract

## Source-Faithful Sleeve Signal
This batch sharply distinguishes between sleeve as structure and active cognition as occupancy.

## Key Points
- nSleeve is a grouped series of nStaks.
- nSleeve is structurally complete but inert.
- A brain/stak installed into a sleeve produces an active entity.
- That active entity is named Envoy in this batch.
- Sleeve therefore does not equal cognition by itself.

## Resleeving / Continuity Signal
The batch does not define a full resleeving protocol, but it strongly implies:
- sleeves are bodies or structural hosts
- cognition can be installed into them
- active identity is not reducible to the inert sleeve alone

## Caution
The batch leaves open whether “Envoy” should remain the live umbrella term once tethered/shared-memory collective naming is finalized.
