## Independent Review
This batch is best treated as a **high-value architecture and governance packet**. It provides one of the clearest whole-system views in the current intake range because it spans philosophy, block roles, stack layout, runtime flow, sleeve identity, bounded agency, multi-agent collaboration, stress testing, and compression format in one source.

Its strongest contribution is the repeated inversion of mainstream alignment framing: alignment here is not described as reward shaping or optimized objective pursuit, but as a **constraint hierarchy** guarded by governance. That signal appears throughout the packet and should be considered a likely synthesis anchor even though Stage 1 should not promote it directly to canon.

The 13-stack architecture is also a major contribution. Even if the specific stack count or naming later changes, this source clearly pushes UMG toward a fully layered system rather than a loose block collection. The addition of `EMERGENCY_FAST_PATH`, recursive audit triggers, load tracking, and ambiguity escalation also gives the batch unusual implementation pressure.

The main caution is absolutism. Several phrases are strong, zero-failure, or invariant-heavy. The source itself acknowledges unresolved questions around who defines non-negotiables, edge-case sovereignty conflicts, cultural variability, and real-time complexity. That means the packet should be merged as evidence of architectural direction, not assumed final settled law.
