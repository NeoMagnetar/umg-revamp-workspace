## Chat-Level Summary
This batch is one of the clearest practical demonstrations of sleeves as engineered runtime artifacts rather than decorative prompt labels. A base Poe sleeve is activated, a catalog sleeve is selected and provenance-traced, a composite sleeve is formed through merge, and a persona overlay is added. When the resulting persona does not inhabit the intended voice reliably, the failure is diagnosed as an architectural error: personality was partially implemented as logic rather than as a dedicated surface actuator. The system then patches the design, introduces a dedicated surface stack, reruns acceptance tests, and version-bumps the sleeve.

## UMG-Relevant Extraction
### 1. Sleeve runtime engineering
- Sleeves are treated as versioned runtime identity containers with stable governance anchoring.
- Sleeves can be activated, provenance-traced, merged, extended, patched, and reissued.
- Sleeve evolution is explicit across versions rather than implicit in untracked prompt drift.

### 2. Merge as capability composition
- The quant/strategic fusion sleeve is presented as a functional merge, not as a loose tone blend.
- The merged sleeve keeps both contributing capabilities legible.
- Merge here is operational: forecast -> evaluate -> fortify.

### 3. Reasoning stacks vs surface stacks
- The summary makes this the key architectural lesson.
- Reasoning stacks control analytic behavior and internal posture.
- Surface stacks control cadence, idiom density, puncture-line behavior, and inhabitation quality.
- Persona failure occurred because surface ownership was missing or misassigned.

### 4. Deterministic persona patching
- The patch process is explicit:
  - identify enactment failure
  - diagnose wrong stack type
  - separate logic and surface concerns
  - add dedicated surface stack
  - acceptance-test the new version
  - intensify surface parameters in a controlled way
- This is strong evidence for deterministic persona engineering rather than informal style prompting.

### 5. Governance continuity
- Governance remains locked to `POE.UMG.CORE`.
- Sleeve changes never imply governance mutation.
- Expression and posture shift under a stable control anchor.

### 6. Orthogonality of format
- `BP_MODE: NL` remains separate from sleeve/stack selection.
- Output format is kept orthogonal to cognition and persona control.
