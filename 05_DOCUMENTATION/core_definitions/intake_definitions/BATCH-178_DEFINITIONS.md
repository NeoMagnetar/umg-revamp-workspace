# BATCH-178 — Definitions Extract

## Terminology Scope Note
These definitions are extracted from a derived recursive-self-improvement summary.
They remain Stage 1 evidence until explicitly promoted elsewhere.

## Core Runtime Terms
**Recursive Self-Scaffolding**  
Applying UMG to its own active structure to propose modular improvements rather than rebuilding wholesale.

**VSS Gate**  
Scoring or threshold mechanism used to identify weak segments and decide whether candidate mutations merit further testing.

**Guarded Merge**  
Simulated or staged merge process in which changes are only accepted if they improve score and pass guardrail validation.

**Sandbox Envoy**  
Temporary isolated actor or sleeve context used to generate and test candidate structural deltas before mainline acceptance.

## Evolution Terms
**Delta Block**  
Candidate structural change, often instruction, philosophy, or blueprint oriented, proposed during a refinement pass.

**Upgrade Bundle**  
Grouped accepted deltas carried forward as a structured improvement package.

## Definitional Candidates
UMG as both workbench and worker.  
observe -> score -> mutate -> validate -> merge.  
Self-improvement by feeding UMG’s own structure back through its own modular pipeline.
