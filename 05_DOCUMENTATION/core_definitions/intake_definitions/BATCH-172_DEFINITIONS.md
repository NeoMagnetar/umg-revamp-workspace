A modular AI framework / architecture for explainable cognition, composable minds, and open innovation at scale.

### Behavioral Blocks
The implied modular behavior units that differentiate UMG from prompt-only or chain-only systems. The source treats them as core, but does not formally define their internal taxonomy.

### Transparent, Portable AI Personalities
A positioning phrase describing UMG as a system for explainable, cross-platform, behavior-stable AI configurations.

### Personality / Behavior Architecture
The market-facing framing that UMG is not just task automation, but a structured layer for configurable AI behavior and identity.

### Trustworthy Governance Layer
A value proposition stating that UMG provides inspectable, controllable behavior where other systems are opaque or hard to govern.

### UMG Personality Format (.umgpersona)
A proposed portable package format for personality/behavior configurations. Mentioned strategically, not established canon.

### Personality Plugin
A proposed integration framing where UMG sits on top of other agent or orchestration systems as the behavior/personality layer.
