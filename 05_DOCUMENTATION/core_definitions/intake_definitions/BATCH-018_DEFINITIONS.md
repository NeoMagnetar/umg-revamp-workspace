# BATCH-018 — Definitions Extract

## Terminology
### MOLT block
Preferred atomic semantic unit. More precise than generic “block” when referring to the lowest working UMG unit.

### NeoBlock
Locally coherent compiled/compressed assembly of MOLT blocks. May contain bundles, mappings, syntheses, and one dominant primary anchor.

### NeoStack
Domain-level assembly of multiple NeoBlocks. Often horizontally composed with possible overlays or supervisory structures above/beside it.

### Sleeve
Current runtime identity containing defaults, constraints, posture, and active stacks.

### Overlay
A shaping or supervisory structure that may sit above or beside stacks without being identical to the reasoning substrate below.

### Route
Active path opened by triggered directives/gates. Shows what is currently being used.

### Structure
What can exist in the system, whether active or not.

## Role Language
- Trigger = gate/opening condition
