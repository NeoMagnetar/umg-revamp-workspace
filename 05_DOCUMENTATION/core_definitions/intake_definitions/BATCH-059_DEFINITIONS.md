# BATCH-059 — Definitions Extract

## Terminology
### UMG / PoeUMG
- **UMG / PoeUMG (usage in this batch):** a modular reasoning scaffold used across policy, diplomacy, forensic analysis, and embodiment design.

### Primary Block / Primary MOLT
- central objective or governing intent for a reasoning pass.
- example from batch: preserve public health while upholding agency and trust.

### Merge Block
- additive support structure supplying facts, constraints, logistics, body mechanics, intelligence inputs, or subsystem modules around a main objective.

### Blueprint Block
- shaping layer governing style, posture, structure, or symbolic expression rather than only factual content.

### Philosophy MOLT
- worldview or ethical lens guiding action.
- example from batch: persuasion over coercion; trust and agency preserved.

### Instruction MOLT
- operational rule or bounded behavior statement.
- example from batch: maximize voluntary uptake; do not initiate coercion.
