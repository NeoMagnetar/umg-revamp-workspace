# BATCH-025 Blocks and MOLT Extract

## MOLT / Block-Level Doctrine
- MOLT block → NeoBlock → NeoStack → Sleeve is reinforced as the core hierarchy.
- Bundle applies to same-type MOLT blocks.
- Gates decide whether a path or bundle becomes active at all.
- Directive-sensitive substructure can exist, but only within higher-order doctrinal bounds.

## Practical Clarifications
- Trigger/gate may open a crisis or situational path.
- That activation does not erase higher doctrine.
- Bundle order is local precedence for grouped same-type blocks.
- NeoBlock may contain both standalone MOLT blocks and Bundles.
