## Role Behavior Extract
- **Primary**: central directive or main task.
- **Subject**: topic, domain, or knowledge orientation.
- **Philosophy**: values, framing, tone, worldview.
- **Instruction**: behavioral or execution constraints.
- **Merge**: combined logic or merged content.
- **Overlay**: temporary context/mode switch.
- **Reflection**: self-check, evaluation, or feedback loop.
- **Blueprint**: identity/scope/memory/structural framing layer, possibly above ordinary block roles.
