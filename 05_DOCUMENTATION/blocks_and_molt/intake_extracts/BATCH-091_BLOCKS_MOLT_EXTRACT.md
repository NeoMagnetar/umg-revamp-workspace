## Role Reading
### Primary
Main directive or central logic mode.

### Subject
Domain or orientation binding.

### Philosophy
Value/tone/worldview layer.

### Instruction
Operational constraint or behavioral limit.

### Merge
Support-logic composition surface.

### Blueprint
Style/identity/scope layer that shapes rendering.

## Stability Notes
- Primary / Subject / Philosophy / Instruction are the most stable recurring role family.
- Blueprint, Meta Layer, and Instruction Node recur, but their exact relationship to the core role family is not fully normalized.
- This batch is therefore useful for comparative taxonomy cleanup rather than direct canon promotion.
