## Captured Meanings
### Template
Core identity or container of thought in one SAFE-style framing.

### Primary
Central idea, directive, or dominant operational mode.

### Subject
Theme, topic, or knowledge/domain orientation.

### Philosophy
Lens, tone, worldview, or logic directive.

### Instruction
Behavioral rule, operational constraint, or limit.

### Blueprint
Intent/style/structure layer or inspiration/root intent surface.

### Merge
Conditional fusion / support-content integration / combination behavior.
