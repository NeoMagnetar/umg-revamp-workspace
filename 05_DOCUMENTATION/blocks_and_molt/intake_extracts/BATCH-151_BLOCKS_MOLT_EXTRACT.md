## Structural Doctrine
Each block must have exactly one `molt_type`.
Role overloading is discouraged.
The source explicitly sharpens the difference among Instruction, Philosophy, Blueprint, and Directive.

## Composition Signal
Blocks are composable, mergeable, and stackable.
Vertical relationships express hierarchy.
Horizontal relationships express overlay or equal scope.
