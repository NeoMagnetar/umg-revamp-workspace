## Role Reading

Defines objective or main directive.

### Subject
Defines focus area or domain.

### Philosophy
Defines behavior lens, tone, or worldview.

### Instruction
Defines execution constraints or behavioral limits.

### Merge
Combines supporting logic and auxiliary detail into the main output.

### Blueprint
Shapes style/format without direct content insertion.
