# BATCH-018 — NeoStructure Extract

## NeoBlock Direction
NeoBlocks should be:
- locally coherent
- not globally exhaustive
- readable in collapsed and expanded form

### Internal schema vocabulary surfaced
- Constituents
- Bundles
- Merges
- Syntheses
- Mappings
- PrimaryAnchor

## NeoStack Direction
NeoStacks should be:
- domain-level assemblies of multiple NeoBlocks
- often horizontally composed from peer NeoBlocks
- optionally shaped by overlays or supervisory blocks above/beside them

## Relationship Distinctions
- merge = rare deep synthesis
- bundle = grouped coordination without identity loss
- overlay = shaping/supervisory layer
- adjacency = side-by-side structure without forced fusion

## Applied Pattern Signal
The dealership and basketball examples reinforce many-specialized-NeoBlocks-per-domain rather than one giant compressed stack object.
